Milestone 7 - Core Combat Resolver

- Added a standalone CombatResolver for canonical actor-owned structured moves.
- Enforced exact move-name ownership, duplicate-name rejection, target semantics,
  supported move boundaries, and unsupported-mechanic rejection.
- Implemented deterministic resolver mechanics for resource spending, accuracy,
  effective-stat scaling, physical/magical/hybrid mitigation, damage, healing,
  actual applied MoveResult values, and persistent Super generation/spending.
- Extended the shared combatant-facing contract so runtime enemies own a fresh
  per-instance Super resource alongside player Super.
- Left the interactive Battle loop unchanged for Milestone 8.
- Recorded that full structured Battle playability for all four Drifters depends
  on additional generic mechanic behavior or later character-kit work.

Milestone 7.5 - Enemy Archetype and Capability Contract

- Added strict enemy rank, role, behavior, and capability metadata.
- Added tier preservation to EnemyState with shared generic tier validation.
- Refactored enemy creation through a registry-driven factory.
- Corrected the ordinary Goblin to COMMON, MELEE_SKIRMISHER, AGGRESSIVE, tier 0,
  BASIC_ATTACKS only, 60 HP, 0 Mana, and the two resolver-supported moves slash
  and jumping slash.
- Deferred suplex and stagger to future higher-complexity Goblin-family
  archetypes.
- Gated resolver Super generation through the shared generates_super combatant
  capability while preserving the approved Milestone 7 formulas and action
  order.
- Documented that the legacy universal enemy healing branch remains temporary
  Battle behavior to remove during Milestone 8 integration.

Milestone 7.6 - Enemy Domain Extraction

- Moved enemy definitions, metadata enums, runtime state, tier validation,
  registration, registry, factory, scaling policy, and authored Goblin content
  from app.combat to the top-level app.enemies domain.
- Split enemy archetype registration from the generic factory and added a
  built-in registry seam for future enemy packages.
- Kept Battle and CombatResolver behavior unchanged while updating imports to
  the new enemy domain.

Milestone 7.7 - Core Defend Contract

- Added a standalone Defend resolver action and per-combatant temporary Defend
  state in CombatState.
- Added Combatant-facing Defend capability and reduction contracts for
  PlayerState and EnemyState.
- Applied Defend reduction after normal resolver mitigation while preserving
  the Milestone 7 damage, healing, accuracy, ownership, and resource order.
- Kept Defend expiration behind accepted-action completion so Battle can wire
  resolution and turn advancement during Milestone 8.
- Left the legacy Battle menu flow, Recover, initiative, enemy AI, player
  loadouts, and Goblin metadata unchanged.
